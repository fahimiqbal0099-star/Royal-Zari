About Royal Zari

Royal Zari is an opulent, centuries-old embroidery technique using gold or silver metallic threads to create luxurious, detailed patterns on fabrics like silk and velvet. Our artisans combine traditional craftsmanship with modern designs to deliver premium quality products.
