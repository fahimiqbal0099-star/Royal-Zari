About Royal Zari - Premium Zari Embroidery Experts

Royal Zari is your premier destination for authentic hand-crafted gold and silver zari embroidery products in Kolkata. We specialize in traditional Indian embroidery techniques, creating luxurious patterns on premium fabrics like silk sarees, velvet dupattas, embroidered kurtas, and wedding lehengas. Our master artisans combine centuries-old craftsmanship with contemporary designs to deliver exceptional quality zari work that tells your unique story.

From intricate gold zari work to delicate silver thread embroidery, we offer a complete range of traditional Indian handicrafts. Whether you're looking for bridal wear, festive attire, or custom embroidery services, Royal Zari brings the rich heritage of Indian textile art to your wardrobe.
