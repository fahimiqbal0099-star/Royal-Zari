## 💰 How to Set Product Prices & Upload Images

### Step 1: Set Your Product Prices
1. Open your website: https://fahimiqbal0099-star.github.io/Royal-Zari/
2. Scroll down to the "📸 Upload Your Product Images" section
3. For each product, enter your desired price in the "Price (₹)" field
4. Click "Update Price" - the price is saved automatically on your website!

### Step 2: Upload Product Images
1. Click "Choose Image" for each product
2. Select your product photo from your device
3. Preview will show instantly on the website
4. Click "Download" to save the image with correct filename

### Step 3: Upload Images to GitHub
1. Go to: https://github.com/fahimiqbal0099-star/Royal-Zari
2. Click "images" folder
3. Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
4. Upload the downloaded images with exact filenames
5. Click "Commit changes"
